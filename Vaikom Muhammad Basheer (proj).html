Vaikom Muhammad Basheer

He was a Malayalam fiction writer. He was a humanist, freedom fighter, novelist and short story writer.

He is noter for his parth-breaking, disarmingly down to earth style of writing that made him equally popular among literary critics as well as the common man.

His notable works include Balyakalasakhi, Shabdangal, Pathummayude Aadu, Mathilukal, Ntuppuppakkorenendarnnu, Janmadinam and Anargha Nimisham.

He was awarded the Padma Shri in 1982. He is fondly remembered as the "Beypore Sultan".
